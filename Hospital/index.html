Our Medical Departments

Florence Hospital provides comprehensive healthcare services through specialized departments equipped with modern medical technology and experienced healthcare professionals committed to delivering quality patient care.

🚑 Emergency Department

Our Emergency Department operates 24 hours a day, providing immediate medical attention for accidents, injuries, and life-threatening conditions with rapid response and professional care.

🩺 Outpatient Department (OPD)

The Outpatient Department offers consultations, medical examinations, diagnosis, treatment, follow-up care, and specialist referrals without requiring hospital admission.

🏥 Inpatient Department

We provide comfortable inpatient accommodation with continuous nursing care, specialist supervision, medication management, and recovery support.

🧪 Laboratory Services

Our laboratory performs accurate diagnostic testing using modern equipment to support timely diagnosis and effective treatment.

💊 Pharmacy

Our pharmacy supplies quality medications, prescription services, patient counseling, and pharmaceutical care under qualified pharmacists.

🩻 Radiology Department

We offer diagnostic imaging services including X-ray, Ultrasound, CT Scan, and other imaging procedures to assist medical diagnosis.

🤱 Maternity Department

Our maternity unit provides antenatal care, safe delivery services, postnatal care, family planning, and newborn support for mothers and babies.

👶 Pediatrics Department

We specialize in healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents, including immunization, nutrition, growth monitoring, and treatment of childhood illnesses.

⚕ Surgery Department

Our surgical team performs both minor and major surgical procedures using modern operating theatres while maintaining the highest safety standards.

🦷 Dental Clinic

We provide preventive and restorative dental care including dental examinations, cleaning, fillings, extractions, and oral health education.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online, by phone, or by visiting the hospital reception.

What documents should I bring?

Please bring a valid identification document, insurance card (if applicable), referral letter (if available), and any previous medical records.

Does the hospital provide emergency services?

Yes. Our Emergency Department operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

Can I access my medical records?

Yes. Patients may request access to their medical records by following the hospital's records request procedure.
